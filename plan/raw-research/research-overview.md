## Research Overview 

### Slot Machine Features: 

#### Speed
- A fast play system, has legal limitations but it good for keeping retention for impatient players or users who only care about money. Provides the user a sence of control. (John)
- The roll should only take 3-5 seconds to keep suspect / exitment for those who want entertainment without being too intrusive to those who don't. (John)
- Include paylines, bonus rounds, pot system, pity system etc... to offer incentives the appeal to a variety of different personas. Entertainment wise it breaks up the monotonay. Economically it gives a "free" reward. Provides reasons (including sunk cost fallacy) to keep playing even if one has been loosing recently (John).


#### Visuals / Audio
- Could include audio to mimic other people also playing, lessens the anxiety of those new to gambling. (John)
- Most slot machines are very saturated in color. Could lean into this rather than the neon asthetic (Sahana)
- Icons are typically a contrasting color to the base machine, but do not contrast as much as we assumed. This may be an attempt to draw less attention for entertainment/experience focused users, keeping them focused on the big animations and the general gameplay loop rather than the "game" aspect.
