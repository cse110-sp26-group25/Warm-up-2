## Slot Machine Jargon

- **RNG (Random Number Generator)**  
  Determines the outcome of each spin randomly and independently.

- **Reels**  
  Vertical columns that spin and display symbols.

- **Paylines**  
  Predefined lines (horizontal, diagonal, zig-zag) where matching symbols result in a win.

- **Symbols**  
  Icons displayed on reels (e.g., fruits, numbers, themed images).

- **Wild Symbol**  
  Substitutes for other symbols to complete winning combinations.

- **Scatter Symbol**  
  Triggers bonuses or free spins regardless of position on paylines.

- **Multiplier**  
  Increases winnings by a factor (e.g., x2, x5).

- **Payout / Paytable**  
  Defines how much each symbol combination is worth.

- **Jackpot**  
  A large prize, sometimes progressive and increases over time.

- **Bet Size**  
  Amount wagered per spin.

- **Autoplay**  
  Automatically spins the reels multiple times without user input.

- **Spin Cycle**  
  The full process from pressing spin to displaying results.

## Visual & Theme Research

### Common Themes

- **Classic Casino**
  - Cherries, 7s, BAR symbols
  - Simple, nostalgic design
  - Minimal animations
  - Appeals to traditional and older users

- **Egyptian**
  - Pharaohs, pyramids, hieroglyphics
  - Gold-heavy color schemes
  - Often includes treasure or bonus features
  - Very common in real casino slots

- **Fantasy**
  - Dragons, magic, mythical creatures
  - Bright, colorful visuals
  - Includes spells, potions, and magical effects
  - Appeals to younger and gaming-oriented users

- **Cyberpunk / Futuristic**
  - Neon colors, glowing symbols
  - Digital grids, AI/robot imagery
  - Fast-paced animations
  - Appeals to tech-savvy users

- **Western**
  - Cowboys, saloons, desert landscapes
  - Earth-tone colors (brown, orange)
  - Themes of gold rush or duels
  - Slower, classic-style gameplay

- **Cartoon / Casual**
  - Bright, playful graphics
  - Exaggerated animations
  - Simple symbols and mechanics
  - Designed for mobile and casual players

- **Adventure / Exploration**
  - Jungle, treasure hunting, lost cities
  - Maps, compasses, artifacts
  - Often includes progression or story elements
  - Encourages longer play sessions

- **Horror / Dark**
  - Vampires, zombies, haunted themes
  - Dark color palettes (black, red, purple)
  - Dramatic sound effects
  - Appeals to niche audiences

- **Space / Sci-Fi**
  - Planets, aliens, spaceships
  - Futuristic UI and glowing effects
  - Often includes high-tech bonus mechanics
  - Popular in modern video slots

- **Luxury / High Roller**
  - Diamonds, gold, expensive items
  - Sleek, polished UI
  - Focus on high-value rewards
  - Appeals to experienced or serious players

- **Sports**
  - Football, racing, boxing themes
  - Team logos or equipment as symbols
  - Competitive feel
  - Appeals to sports fans

- **Seasonal / Event-Based**
  - Christmas, Halloween, holidays
  - Limited-time visuals and themes
  - Bright and festive design
  - Encourages engagement through novelty
