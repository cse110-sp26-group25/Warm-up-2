📖 How to Play

Basics

  • The machine has 3 rows and 5 columns — 15 symbols visible per spin.
  • There are 9 paylines. A win occurs when 3 or more identical symbols appear consecutively from the left on any payline.
  • Select a bet (5–500 credits) using the chip buttons, then click SPIN or press Space / Enter.
  • The 🤖 Robot is a Wild — it substitutes for any non-wild symbol.
  • Land five Robots on the middle row to win the progressive jackpot.
  • Win amounts are the payout multiplier × your bet. Multiple winning paylines add together.
